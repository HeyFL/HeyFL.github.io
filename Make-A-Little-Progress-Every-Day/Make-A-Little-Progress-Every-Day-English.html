Vocabulary

Sentence - conversation

  1. Just updating is enough. (只要更新就够了)
  2. I call handle it all by myself, expect business matters. (我自己处理所有事情，除了商务事务)
  3. I am swamped. (我忙得不可开交)

Sentence - email

  1. I am here to assist you with anything you need. (我在这里协助你处理任何你需要的事情)
  2. I am writing to inform you that… (我写信是为了通知你…)
  3. Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns. (如果你有任何问题或疑虑，请告诉我)
  4. I look forward to hearing from you soon. (我期待很快收到你的回复)
  5. Thank you for your time and consideration. (感谢你的时间和考虑)
